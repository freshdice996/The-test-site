Tribute to Robert Chandler: (4 September 1927 – 6 September 2011)

  • Robert "Bob" Franklin Chandler Jr. known for many things includng his "work hard, play hard" lifestyle.
  • Bob served in the United States Army as a part of the Signal Corps after graduating from Auburn University with a degree in aeronautical engineering.
  • After being stationed in Korea, Bob returned to the states to pursue more college education and eventually landed a position working for Western Electric where he worked for 40 years.
  • In his downtime, Bob was known to take up various hobbies including but not limited to: gardening, reading, and even performing karaoke at bars and pubs.
  • Bob's musical influence did not stop at karaoke nights, as he was an avid collector of jazz vinyl records and would occasionally host on radio as a discjockey while providing knowledge and lore about the rarities he would broadcast.
